Invitation and Greetings
Dear colleague,
As coordinator of the Organizing Committee of the 15th International Conference on Lactoferrin Structure, Function and Applications, I would like to invite you to this year’s virtual conference at Beijing. The conference will be held online on December 4-6 of 2021. This virtual conference online is a special one at a special time under situation of wide-spread of Covid-19, which will be of unique significance in the history of the Lactoferrin Conference.
Since you have a special interest in lactoferrin or have attended previous conferences, we would like to invite you to participate in the 2021 Lactoferrin Conference and present your research progress and view sharing. It will be a challenge with regard to different time zones, but the wonderful academic exchange on lactoferrin is not to be missed, looking forward to your participation!
Kind regards,
Jianhua Wang, PhD
Coordinator, 15th International Conference on Lactoferrin (Virtual/online, Beijing 2021)
Professor and PI, Feed Research Institute, Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences,
China; wangjianhua@caas.cn; wangjianhua.peking@qq.com
(By Jianhua Wang, 8.8.2021, Beijing, END )
